About CogSMART‑Z

CogSMART‑Z adapts evidence-based, strategy-first cognitive rehabilitation to help people strengthen memory, attention, learning, and executive skills so they can succeed at work, school, and daily life.

Resources

What it is

CogSMART‑Z is a practical, clinician-friendly set of strategies and tools that teach compensatory techniques for everyday cognitive problems. The program emphasizes simple steps users can apply in real-world situations and practice across sessions.

How it works

  • Teach concrete, evidence-based strategies (prospective memory, retrospective memory, attention)
  • Practice in real-life tasks and track progress
  • Use self-guided lessons or clinician-assigned modules

Evidence & outcomes

The CogSMART approach is grounded in clinical research and has been adapted for multiple populations (including traumatic brain injury, psychiatric illness, and mild cognitive impairment). Studies show improvements in strategy use and daily functioning when interventions are delivered with fidelity.

Program materials and manuals support clinicians and self-guided users alike.
